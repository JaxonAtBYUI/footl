Overview

Purpose

Have you heard of Ted Bundy, JonBenét Ramsey, or D.B. Cooper? Do you know their full stories? Finding all the nitty gritty details on these popular cases is hard to do. My consultant and I aim to make a one stop shop for the cold hard facts on some of the most infamous true crime cases. With sources and information presented in an easy to digest way, gone are the days of scouring the internet high and low for your information. Come visit our website and learn about these famous cases and more!

Audience

Anyone interested in or curious about true crime. The content will be formatted in such a way that understanding the cases will be easy for those who are not familiar with the cases. The links to the sources of information will be available and easily accessible at the end of each article for anybody who wants to dig deeper, fact check, or cite the article. This way it is a place for those who are new to the case or who are already familiar.

Ted Bundy, as he is known, is one of the most infamous serial killers in the United States and is often referred to as the American Boogeyman. With twenty confirmed killings, and a confession of up to thirty kills, it is no wonder why his name is widely recognized across the United States. Blog

Zodiac Killer Found?

Recent news of the Zodiac Killer’s identity being discovered has broke out. It has been reported by Fox news that his name is Gary F. Poste. This conclusion was reached by a group of people called the Case Breakers who determined that he was the Zodiac Killer by evidence and photos found in Poste’s dark room. In these photographs, it was revealed that he had a scar on his forehead that matched the sketch of the Zodiac Killer. This evidence dubious however. Attempts have been made to connect to other cases such as the Bates Case, but it was concluded that they were not connected. The Authorities have rebuffed the Case Breakers and have stated that their evidence is mostly circumstantial. The waters are murky but it seems likely at a real conclusion to this case will not come soon.

Gabby Petito Updates

The Gabby Petito case has gained a lot of traction in world of social media. Since her disappearance in August 2021, where she was last seen with her boyfriend. People have been following the case closely and demanding justice for her. Following her disappearance there have been major search parties to find her boyfriend and to search for her. Consequently, many other missing persons cases have been solved, and the remains of victims have been recovered. The solving of these cases has garnered new awareness for missing persons and domestic abuse. In a recently released bodycam video from a Utah police officer, it was revealed that Gabby Petito was exhibiting signs of abuse from her boyfriend Brian Laundrie. As a result, of this footage and the Petito case, the public has become more educated on domestic abuse. There have been videos made about how to handle domestic abuse or how to signal for help if you find yourself in these situations. With the recovery of the bodies it just goes to show that people do give on missing person cases if they do not strike the hearts of the masses.
