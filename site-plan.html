Overview

Purpose

Have you heard of Ted Bundy, JonBen√©t Ramsey, or D.B. Cooper? Do you know their full stories? Finding all the nitty gritty details on these popular cases is hard to do. My consultant and I aim to make a one stop shop for the cold hard facts on some of the most infamous true crime cases. With sources and information presented in an easy to digest way, gone are the days of scouring the internet high and low for your information. Come visit our website and learn about these famous cases and more!

Audience

Anyone interested in or curious about true crime. The content will be formatted in such a way that understanding the cases will be easy for those who are not familiar with the cases. The links to the sources of information will be available and easily accessible at the end of each article for anybody who wants to dig deeper, fact check, or cite the article. This way it is a place for those who are new to the case or who are already familiar.
